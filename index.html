Hello

I'm a 27yo self-taught front-end dev from Cornwall/England that now resides here in Stockholm/Sweden. I started my coding journey late 2016 and as of now I am mainly focused on front-end development, however this isn't my final form, my goal is to become a full-stack developer!

In my spare time, I have taught myself HTML, CSS, SCSS and Bootstrap, along with some JavaScript, jQuery and Git. I'll admit I've still got a lot to learn, but that's what's so exciting, there's so much to discover!
What am I up at the moment? Lots! I'm dipping my toes into the world of React which is awesome and super enjoyable to use, I love a good challenge so It's been fun trying to get my head around it. The possibilities are endless!

What do I do when I'm not pushing code? Well you can find me playing Rugby with the Stockholm Berserkers, playing computer games, drinking tea, catching up on my reading material and out walking/jogging with my dog.
